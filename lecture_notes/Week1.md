## What is this class? 
This is a 1  hour seminar acting as an introduction to the major, interesting issues and related research, and the avenues available to students who attain a degree in that major.

We will cover:
* Research and academic ethics.
* Career pathways.
* Study habits.
* The application of the major to current issues and careers.
